About Us

Charles Matteliano

Chuck's was founded in October 1974 by Charles Matteliano and is presently owned and operated by his son, Eddie Matteliano.

In our 45 years in Hamilton we have called a few places home. In 2007 we moved from our last location on Main St. & Blake St. to our current location 804 King St. E Hamilton, Ontario.

Showing our dedication to our community and being able to give back is a storied tradition at Chuck's. We are proud to have served and continue to serve our community by providing great, affordable service, sponsoring sports teams and community events.

At Chuck's customer satisfaction is our greatest asset and we go to great length to keep you driving safely and coming back to us for all your service needs.

So if your car is acting up, or not sounding right - bring it in and we will be happy to "look under the hood" for you.
